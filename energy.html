Energy Quantization

Energy levels inside a box follow:

Eₙ ∝ n²

This means: - Energy increases rapidly as n increases - Energy levels are NOT equally spaced

This arises from boundary conditions: ψ(0) = 0 and ψ(L) = 0

This is a direct consequence of solving Schrödinger’s equation.
