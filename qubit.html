From Particle to Qubit

A qubit is formed using two quantum states:

|0⟩ = ground state (n=1) |1⟩ = excited state (n=2)

A general state:

|ψ⟩ = a|0⟩ + b|1⟩

Unlike classical bits, qubits exist in superposition.

This allows parallel computation — the basis of quantum computing.
