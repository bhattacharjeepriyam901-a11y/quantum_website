From Particle in a Box to Qubit

As modern electronic devices shrink to the nanoscale, classical physics fails to explain particle behavior, requiring a quantum mechanical approach. This project investigates how spatial confinement leads to energy quantization using the particle in a box model. It further explores dynamic quantum behavior through superposition and time evolution. By mapping these states onto a Bloch sphere, the study demonstrates how fundamental quantum principles give rise to qubits and modern quantum technologies.
